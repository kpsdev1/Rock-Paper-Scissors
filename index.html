How to Play

Select your hand from the bottom of the page either Rock, Paper or Scissors. The symbols are Rock, Paper, Scissors in that order from left to right. When you choose your hand the computer will simultaneously choose their hand.

Rules
1. Rock beats Scissors.
2. Paper Beats Rock.
3. Scissors beats Paper.
First to 5 wins the Game.

The players hand will be shown on the left while the computers hand will be shown on the right, before starting each will show a question mark.
